Wide area bonjour (WAB)/ Wide area SDG

Service Discovery Gateway (SDG) is used for discovering services across VLANs, which is not possible with mDNS protocol as mDNS is a link local protocol. SDG solution for wired networks is available from over past 8 years and it is deployed across the world.
But with BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) and increase in service providing devices like Apple TV, laptops, music services, printers and various other IOT devices, it has became quite cumbersome for an IT administrator in an enterprise to monitor and enforce device policies. Monitoring services in such huge network is just not manageable. Also changing service policies is another nightmare. Sometimes it takes a month to configure across. Also without insight into what kind of services network is hosting, its not possible to derive any policy decisions. Similar examples exist in other enterprises, as well as in schools and universities.
To solve this problem, we, at Cisco developed an enterprise solution which gives service monitoring and 100s of other features. This makes the network administrator's job so easy that it just takes a few clicks to push any policy and give powerful control to change network rules to meet emerging user needs. More info

Solution Architecture

This solution consists of several SDG's and APICEM/DNAC (Cloud controller). Each SDG maintains a secure connection with the controller to exchange services over BCP (Bonjour control protocol).
